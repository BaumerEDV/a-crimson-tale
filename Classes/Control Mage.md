# Abilities
## Always Available
### Magic Missile
Action: Main Action
Effect: d4 damage, strikes unerringly.
### Ray of Frost
Action: Main Action
Attack Roll vs AC
Hit: d8 damage, target's speed is halved until the start of your next turn.
### Confound
Action: Main Action
Mental Save
Fail: The target becomes distracted; attacks against it have +2 to hit until the start of your next turn.

## Per Scene
### Blindness
Effort: spend Effort for the scene
Action: Main Action
Will Save
Fail: Target is blinded until the start of your next turn.
### Grease
Effort: spend Effort for the scene
Action: Main Action
Effect: Create a 10x10 foot square patch of grease on the floor within 90 feet. Creatures inside the area when it appears, creatures that enter the area, and creatures that end their turn in the area must succeed on a Reflex Save or fall prone.
### Command: Halt!
Effort: spend Effort for the scene
Action: Main Action
Will Save
Fail: The target does nothing until the start of your next turn.
### Earthbind
Effort: spend Effort for the scene
Action: Main Action
Physical Save
Fail: The target gets pulled to the ground and cannot fly, float or similar. Repeat the save at the start of every one of your turns to end the effect.
### Feather Fall
Effort: spend Effort for the scene
Action: Reaction, when you see one or more creatures or things falling.
Effect: Slow their rate of descent to 30 feet per round. Affected creatures can control their direction while descending and choose when to descend during their turn in combat.

## Per Expedition
### Sleep
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Main Action
Effect: In a 20 foot radius sphere within 90 feet, 2d8 HD worth of creatures fall asleep for an hour. Creatures with lower HD are affected first. Cannot affect creatures with more than 4 HD. Does not affect creatures that do not need to sleep. As a Main Action, a creature adjacent to a sleeping creature can either wake them up or unerringly kill them.
This ability provokes Attacks of Opportunity.
### Fog Cloud
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Main Action
Effect: In a 30 foot radius sphere within 120 feet, an opaque cloud of fog appears and remains there for the remainder of the scene - unless dispersed by strong winds.
This ability provokes Attacks of Opportunity.
### Color Spray
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Main Action
Effect: Pick up to 3 creatures in a 60 foot cone on front of you. They must succeed on a Physical Save or be blinded for the remainder of the scene.
This ability provokes Attacks of Opportunity.
### Hold Monster
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Main Action
Will Save
Fail: Creature is paralyzed. Repeats the save at the start of your turns. 
This ability provokes Attacks of Opportunity.
### Counterspell
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Reaction, when you see a spell being cast
Effect: It isn't.
### Dispel Magic
Effort: spend Effort for the Expedition
Action: Main Action
Effect: End a magical effect within 150 feet. Some permanent enchantments are too strong to be ended this way; these might either be suppressed for a minute or utterly unaffected - some might require a check. If an effect is utterly immune to being dispelled, you regain your Effort, but not your action.
